The ozone layer acts as a shield to protect the Earth from the Sun's harmful UV rays
Without ozone, we would get more diseases such as skin cancer, cataracts and impaired immune systems.
Thanks to human's pollution, a hole has formed in the ozone layer. This means that the Sun's UV rays can come to Earth and we will be more suspectible to diseases

How do I protect it?

If you're going to go the supermarket, avoid cars, instead travel on bicycles and public transport. Cars produce nitrogen oxide, a gas which can weaken the ozone layer.
You can buy local products near your house because the more you travel using your car, the weaker the ozone layer gets
During hot weather, take care of your air conditioners as their malfunctions cause CFC to escape into the atmosphere.
